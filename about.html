About Spotly

Connecting communities with trusted skilled workers and businesses.

Our Story

Spotly was created with one simple mission: to help people easily discover skilled workers and businesses around them.

Every day, millions of talented workers struggle to find customers, while customers struggle to find trusted service providers nearby. We believed there should be a better way.

Instead of endless scrolling through entertainment content, Spotly was built as a platform that helps people discover real skills, real businesses, and real opportunities in their local communities.

Our Mission

  • Empower local businesses to grow online.
  • Give skilled workers digital visibility.
  • Make service discovery simple and location-based.
  • Create economic opportunities for millions of people.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading local skills discovery platform where anyone can easily find trusted workers and businesses anywhere.

Our Community

Spotly is built for customers, skilled workers, businesses, agents, and communities.

We serve plumbers, tailors, mechanics, electricians, hairdressers, builders, farmers, designers, artisans, caterers, photographers, barbers, bakers, and any person with a skill or service to offer.

We believe every skill deserves to be discovered.
