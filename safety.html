Safety Tips

Your safety is our priority.

Review provider profiles carefully.
Read ratings and customer reviews.
Meet in safe locations when possible.
Avoid making full payments before work begins.
Ask for receipts or written agreements.
Report suspicious behavior to Spotly.
Verify identity documents when necessary.
Keep communication within trusted channels.
Emergency Notice
If you feel unsafe or suspect fraud, stop the transaction immediately and contact local authorities.
