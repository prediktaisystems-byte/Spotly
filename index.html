About Spotly

Connecting communities with trusted skilled workers and businesses.

Our Story

Spotly was created with one simple mission: to help people easily discover skilled workers and businesses around them.

Every day, millions of talented workers struggle to find customers, while customers struggle to find trusted service providers nearby. We believed there should be a better way.

Instead of endless scrolling through entertainment content, Spotly was built as a platform that helps people discover real skills, real businesses, and real opportunities in their local communities.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Last Updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

  • Name and phone number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Business information and occupation
  • Location information (town, LGA, state, area)
  • Uploaded photos and videos
  • Identity verification documents

How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your business profile to potential customers
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Verify provider identities
  • Communicate important updates
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

Location Information

Spotly uses your location to show nearby providers, display providers on maps, and improve search results. Location data is used only for platform functionality.

Photos and Videos

Photos and videos uploaded by providers may be displayed publicly on their profile and within Spotly features. You control what you upload.

Identity Verification

Verification documents are used solely for identity verification and are never publicly displayed. They are reviewed by the Spotly team only.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Maps — for location and mapping features
  • Google Drive — for photo and document storage
  • Flutterwave — for payment processing
  • Notification providers for alerts

Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage to improve performance, save your location preferences, and speed up loading times. No advertising cookies are used.

Data Protection

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information. Your data is never sold to third parties or used for advertising purposes.

Your Rights

  • Update your profile information at any time
  • Request account deletion by contacting us
  • Ask questions about how your data is used

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@spotly.ng

3. Provider Responsibilities

Spotly may suspend or remove accounts that violate these Terms and Conditions, without prior notice in serious cases.

Community Guidelines

To keep Spotly safe, honest, and trustworthy for everyone.

Be Respectful

Treat all users, providers, and agents with professionalism and courtesy. Harassment, insults, or abusive language will not be tolerated.

Be Honest

Use real, accurate information about yourself and your business. Misleading profiles harm the community and will be removed.

No Spam

Do not send unwanted messages, post irrelevant content, or create misleading listings. Spam accounts will be permanently removed.

No Fraud

Fraudulent accounts, fake identities, and deceptive practices are strictly prohibited. Such accounts will be permanently removed and reported.

Respect Reviews

Do not manipulate ratings or create fake reviews. Reviews must be based on genuine experiences. Only one review per provider is allowed per device.

Upload Appropriate Content

Photos and videos must relate to your business or skills. Offensive, explicit, or unrelated content will be removed immediately.

Protect Your Community

If you notice suspicious activity, fake profiles, or harmful content, report it to Spotly at support@spotly.ng
