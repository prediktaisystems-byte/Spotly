Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about Spotly.

What is Spotly?
Spotly is a platform that connects users with trusted workers and businesses nearby.
Is Spotly free to use?
Yes. Searching and contacting providers is completely free.
What is a "Verified ID" badge?
It shows a provider has submitted valid government-issued identification (ID + selfie) that Spotly's team has reviewed and approved. It confirms identity only — not the quality of their work.
How do I get a "Verified ID" badge?
Go to your Provider Dashboard → Verify ID (Verification Center), then upload a clear photo of your government-issued ID and a selfie, and submit. Spotly's admin team reviews it and updates your status within 24–48 hours.
I can't see the "Verified ID badge" option in settings. Why?
This option only appears for registered providers. If you're a customer account, you won't see it — verification is provider-only. If you are a provider and still don't see it, log out and back in, or make sure your provider profile setup is complete.
What should I do to pass KYC verification?
Upload a valid, unexpired government-issued ID with a clear, unedited photo. Upload a well-lit selfie of yourself with your face clearly visible, matching your ID. Make sure both images aren't blurry and nothing is cropped out.
How long is this badge valid?
Once approved, the Verified ID badge stays active on your profile and doesn't expire on its own. Spotly can revoke it if documents are later found to be invalid or fraudulent.
I submitted my ID and selfie, but I didn't get a badge. What should I do?
Reviews take 24–48 hours. If it's been longer, check your Verification Center for a rejection reason, such as a blurry photo or a selfie that doesn't match your ID, and resubmit after correcting it. If it's still unresolved, contact Spotly support.
What is a Premium badge?
It shows a provider is on Spotly's paid Premium Plan (₦2,000/month), which boosts visibility in search results and on the map, and unlocks extra features like video portfolio uploads.
How do I get a premium badge?
From your Provider Dashboard, tap "Upgrade to Premium — ₦2,000/month" and complete payment. Your badge and expiry date update immediately after payment is confirmed.
What are the prohibited activities on Spotly?
1. Impersonating other users or providers
2. Uploading illegal or harmful content
3. Committing fraud or misrepresentation
4. Harassing other users
5. Manipulating ratings or creating fake reviews
Should I chat or call the provider?
Spotly doesn't have in-app chat. You contact providers directly by Call or WhatsApp from their profile, with no middleman.
How long will my premium ads stay on Spotly?
Your Premium Plan runs for 30 days from payment and expires automatically at the end of that period unless renewed.
How do I renew my Spotly Premium Plan?
Go to your Provider Dashboard and tap "Upgrade to Premium" again before or after it expires. Payment extends your plan by another 30 days.
How often can I renew my Premium Plan?
As often as you like. You can renew any time, even before the current cycle ends.
What if my Premium Plan is closed?
Your profile drops back to the Free plan. You lose the Premium badge, priority placement, and video portfolio access, but your listing stays active and reachable.
How to leave a review about the provider?
Open the provider's profile, tap the review/rating option, choose a star rating, and write your feedback. Only one review per provider per device is allowed.
How to search a provider on Spotly?
Use the search bar on the home or search screen and type a service, name, or category. You can also switch to Map mode to browse providers near you.
How do I share my photos on the For You page?
Go to Dashboard → My Profile → Portfolio, tap "Add Photos" and upload from your device. Tap the Share/Live button on a photo to open the "Share to For You" sheet, add an optional caption and hashtags, then tap "Post to For You." Free plan: up to 3 photo slots. Premium: up to 10 photo slots.
How do I share my videos on the For You page?
This is a Premium-only feature. Go to Dashboard → My Profile → Videos and tap "Add Portfolio Video," paste a video link from YouTube, Facebook, TikTok or Instagram, add a title/caption, and save (up to 3 videos can be uploaded to your portfolio). Tap "Share to For You" on any uploaded video to publish it — you can have up to 3 videos live on the For You feed at once. Tap "Share Again" to repost, or "Remove" to free up a slot when full.
How long do my For You posts stay visible?
Free Plan: photo posts stay live for 7 days, then expire automatically. No video posts allowed. Premium Plan: both photo and video posts stay live for 30 days, then expire automatically unless reposted, with up to 10 photo slots and 3 video slots at once.
How do I find a provider's business location on the map?
Open a provider's post on the For You feed and tap "Location," or go to Maps from the bottom nav and select a provider to open a full map with their live pin. Tap "Open in Google Maps" or "Get Directions" to navigate.
How do I view my business location on the map?
Go to Dashboard → My Profile and tap "View my business on Map" to preview your pin exactly as customers see it. If no location is set, tap "Update Business Location" to add one.
How do I share my business location with customers?
In your Provider Dashboard, find the location preview section and tap "Share Location" to open your device's share sheet (or WhatsApp) with a Google Maps directions link plus your Spotly profile link. Or tap "Copy Link" to copy the location link directly.
How do customers get directions to my business?
From your profile or a For You post, they tap "Open in Google Maps" or "Get Directions" for instant turn-by-turn navigation to your pin.
How do I share my business profile?
Go to Dashboard → Share Profile and tap "Share" to open your device's native share sheet (or WhatsApp) with your profile link.
How do I update my business profile?
Go to Dashboard → My Profile and edit your bio, occupation, and location directly, then save. To change your name or phone number, contact Spotly support.
How do I upload photos to my profile?
Go to Dashboard → My Profile → Portfolio and tap "Add Photos" to select images from your device. Free plan allows up to 3 portfolio photos.
How do I upload videos to my profile?
This is Premium only. Go to Dashboard → My Profile → Videos and tap "Add Portfolio Video," paste your YouTube/Facebook/Instagram link, add a title/description, and save. Up to 3 videos can be uploaded and featured on your public profile.
How do I contact Spotly?
Open the menu → Contact Us, fill in your name, subject (Complaint, Account Issue, Payment Issue, Provider Issue, General Inquiry, Business Enquiry), and message, then tap "Send Message." Spotly aims to respond within 24 hours on business days.
How do I report a problem or bug?
Go to Contact Us, select the closest matching subject (e.g. Account Issue), describe the bug clearly, and submit. Use this also for suspicious activity, fake profiles, or harmful content.
